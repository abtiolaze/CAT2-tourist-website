Diani Beach: The biggest beach in Mombasa, Diani Beach is known for its soft white sand, swaying palm trees, and shimmering ocean. It's a great place for swimming, sunbathing, and watersports.




Haller Park: , also known as Bamburi Nature Trail, is a nature park located in Mombasa, kenya. The park is now home to a variety of plant and animal species, including giraffes, zebras, crocodiles, ostriches etc. Haller Park is a popular tourist destination, and visitors can enjoy a variety of activities, including game drives, boat rides, and nature walks.


The Mombasa Marine National Park and Reserve: is a beautiful marine park located near Mombasa, Kenya. It's one of the most popular marine parks in Kenya, and for good reason! The park protects a stunning area of coral reefs, teeming with colorful fish and other marine life.
